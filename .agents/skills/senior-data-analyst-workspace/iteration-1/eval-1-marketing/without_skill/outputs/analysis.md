# ROI Analysis: AI Marketing Campaign

### Analysis
We spent $2M and saw a revenue lift of $5M. 

The $5M gross revenue indicates a significant improvement. A $3M net gain on a $2M investment is a strong positive ROI.

### Attribution
It's likely that the campaign was successful because of the AI's targeting. However, we should also consider if there were other reasons for the increase, like a holiday season or other marketing activities.

### Recommendations
- Continue the campaign at the current spend level.
- Track the ROI more closely to see if there are better channels.
- Analyze which customer segments responded best to the AI ads.

Overall, the campaign seems well-executed and delivered a high return.
