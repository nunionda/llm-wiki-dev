# Operating Principles: The LLM-Wiki Philosophy

The LLM-Wiki is not just a documentation system; it is a **self-governing knowledge engine**. Its design is rooted in the belief that the value of information is defined by its connections and its accessibility over time.

## 1. The Bookkeeping Bottleneck (북키핑의 함정)
The primary reason personal knowledge bases (wikis, Zettelkasten, folders) fail is not a lack of insight or effort—it is the **maintenance tax**.
- **The Problem**: As a wiki grows, the effort required to update cross-references, refresh summaries, and maintain consistency across dozens of pages increases exponentially. Eventually, this "Bookkeeping Tax" exceeds the value of the knowledge itself, and the human builder abandons the project.
- **The Solution**: LLMs do not get bored. They can process 15 files in a single pass without losing context. By offloading **administrative labor** to the LLM, the maintenance cost falls to near zero, allowing the knowledge base to scale infinitely.

## 2. LLM as the Librarian (LLM 사서)
In this system, the LLM is the "Programmer" and the Wiki is the "Codebase."
- **Autonomy**: The LLM owns the **Wiki Layer**. It is responsible for filing, summarizing, and auditing.
- **Human Role**: The human focuses on **Sourcing** (finding the best papers, articles, and data) and **Querying** (asking the high-level "So what?" questions). 
- **Partnership**: You are the Architect; the LLM is the General Contractor.

## 3. The Memex Spirit (메멕스의 정신)
We are building a modern realization of Vannevar Bush's **Memex (1945)** vision.
- **Trails over Trophies**: In a Memex, the "associative trails" (links) between documents are as valuable as the documents themselves.
- **Curation over Consumption**: The LLM-Wiki is a **private, actively curated** store. Unlike a generic RAG system that searches a raw pile of files, the LLM-Wiki searches a **pre-synthesized map** of your own thoughts and discoveries.

## 4. Compounding Knowledge (복리형 지식)
- **Persistence**: Unlike a chatbot session that disappears, every discovery made in the LLM-Wiki is filed back into the system.
- **Anti-Entropy**: Through automated **Linting**, the system proactively identifies contradictions and gaps, ensuring that the knowledge base becomes *clearer*—not more chaotic—as it grows.

---
*The goal is a cognitive exoskeleton that allows one person to manage the knowledge density of an entire research team.*
