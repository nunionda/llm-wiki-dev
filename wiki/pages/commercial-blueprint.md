## 2. Competitive MOAT: The Vibe Engine

Our core IP is not the data storage, but the **Intent Retainment Engine**.

1. **Vibe Ingest (Ghost Mode)**: Silently observing developer/manager "vibes" to populate knowledge.
2. **Deterministic Cross-linking**: Reducing "Hallucination" by grounding links in architectural guardrails.
3. **Pattern Replication**: Exporting "Best Patterns" as reusable templates across projects.

## 3. Product Roadmap

### **Stage 1: The Insight Core (Q2 2026)**
- MVP launch with `hybrid` governance and Nordic Pro UI.
- Direct repository integration (GitHub/GitLab).

### **Stage 2: The Collaboration Layer (Q3 2026)**
- Multi-user "Vibe Collaboration" (Real-time intent sync).
- Mobile "Field Ingest" (Analog-to-Vibe scanning).

### **Stage 3: The Autonomous Economy (2027)**
- **Vibe-Market**: Trade verified "Knowledge Patterns" for crypto/credits.
- Internal "Marketplace" for departments to share expertise via SLM agents.
