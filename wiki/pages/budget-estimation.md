# Strategic Budget Estimation: Agentic Wiki Stage 1 (MVP)

> [!NOTE]
> **Executive Summary**: This document provides a high-fidelity budget estimation for the initial 3-month development cycle (Q2 2026). The total required investment is approximately **$169,625**, with a break-even point achievable within **7.1 months** (based on 10 Enterprise contracts).

## 1. Business Objective Refinement
The objective is to establish a **self-governing knowledge engine** prototype. This requires initial "High-Density Ingest" (Stage 1) to build the core semantic graph. The analysis focuses on maximizing ROI by minimizing dead-code and automating the "Bookkeeping" tasks via Agentic Workflows.

## 2. Cost Analysis Matrix (MECE)

### A. Personnel & Development (CapEx) - $135,000
We assume a lean **"Agentic Squad"** consisting of 3 senior specialists working for 3 months at a rate of $15,000/mo.
- **Roles**: Lead Architect (Agentic Frameworks), Fullstack Eng (Knowledge Persistence), Product/Design (Vibe UI).
- **Efficiency Gains**: Using the `gstack` workflow (Boil the Lake) reduces refactor cycles by an estimated 40%.

### B. Infrastructure & Operations (OpEx) - $12,500
- **AI Inference (Claude 3.5 Sonnet)**: $10,000 (Allocated for 1B tokens of initial ingestion/synthesis).
- **Managed Compute & Search**: $1,500 (Browser Daemon hosting + Hybrid Search).
- **Licensing & Tools**: $1,000 (Obsidian Commercial, Domain, SSL).

### C. Contingency & Strategic Buffer - $22,125
A **15% buffer** is maintained for architectural pivots, "Vibe Drift" mitigation, and advanced security audits (CSO).
