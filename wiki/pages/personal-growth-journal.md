## 2. Mindset & Reflection Log

### Core Mindset Principles

1. **Compound Growth**: Small daily habits compound into transformative results
2. **Reflection Before Action**: Journal first, then build — reflection prevents premature optimization
3. **Balance Seeking**: Technical depth without personal health is unsustainable
4. **Reading as Input**: Every reading session produces a reflection — knowledge only compounds when processed
