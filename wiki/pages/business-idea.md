# Business Idea: AI-Native Agentic Wiki

This page outlines the first business expansion idea derived from the Agentic Wiki POC: **Knowledge Compounding as a Service**.

## 1. Core Concept
Transforming the current internal POC into a commercial enterprise solution where the "Vibe Engine" automatically handles the mapping of human intent to organizational knowledge.

## 2. Competitive Moat (Vibe Coding)
- **Zero-Latency Ingest**: Moving from manual entry to background "vibe capturing".
- **Semantic Relationship Discovery**: Automatically linking disparate business silos.
- **Hybrid Governance**: Importance-based review flow for C-level alignment.
