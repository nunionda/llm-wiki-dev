# Agentic Strategy

The **Agentic Strategy** defines how Vibe Coding reaches its full potential in an enterprise environment by shifting from static documentation to a dynamic, compounding knowledge asset.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Hybrid Governance**: This document is marked as `hybrid`. Any AI-generated synthetic updates will remain in `pending_review` until a human architect approves the "Vibe" shift.

## 1. Intent Capturing
Focus on the "Why" (Intent) rather than just the "What" (Output). This enables AI agents to make decisions aligned with the original architectural vision even long after the human architect has moved on.

## 2. Semantic Mapping
The Agentic Core automatically maps new requirements (Vibes) to existing patterns. This prevents "Vibe Drift" where the product architecture becomes fragmented over time.

## 3. The Connective Tissue
AI-Native acts as the bridge between:
- **The Expert**: Setting the "Patterns" and "Guardrails".
- **The Citizen Architect**: Executing business requirements within those boundaries.
